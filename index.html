ZAS Library Rules

  • Return borrowed books within 2 weeks. Extensions may be requested at the librarian's desk.
  • Handle all books with care. Report any damaged books immediately to Ms. Agnis.
  • Maintain a quiet atmosphere in the library. Use indoor voices for discussions.
  • No food or drinks are allowed near books. Water bottles with secure caps are permitted.
  • Students may borrow up to 3 books at a time with a valid student ID.
  • Lost or severely damaged books must be paid for at replacement cost.
  • Keep mobile devices on silent mode. Take phone calls outside the library.
  • Return chairs and tables to their original positions before leaving.
  • Respect other students' study space and maintain appropriate conduct at all times.
  • Use bookmarks only—never fold pages or place books face-down to mark your place.
